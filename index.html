Lunch Recipes

Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced

Procedure:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and chicken, cook until browned.
  3. Add vegetables and soy sauce, stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with rice.

Vegetable Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese

Procedure:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Sauté vegetables in olive oil for 5 minutes.
  3. Mix with drained pasta and cheese.
  4. Serve warm.

Beef Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Lettuce, cheese, and salsa for topping

Procedure:

  1. Cook beef and onion in a pan until browned.
  2. Add cumin and season to taste.
  3. Fill taco shells with mixture and toppings.
  4. Serve immediately.

Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Add eggs and milk, stir until smooth.
  4. Pour into greased pan and bake for 35 minutes.
  5. Let cool before serving.

Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 apples, diced
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 orange, segmented
  • 2 tbsp honey

Procedure:

  1. Combine all fruits in a bowl.
  2. Drizzle with honey.
  3. Mix gently and chill for 30 minutes.
  4. Serve cold.

Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 200g cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup graham cracker crust

Procedure:

  1. Prepare crust in a pan.
  2. Beat cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Pour over crust and bake at 325°F for 50 minutes.
  4. Cool and refrigerate before serving.

Appetizer Recipes

Caesar Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing

Procedure:

  1. Wash and chop lettuce.
  2. Toss lettuce with dressing.
  3. Add croutons and cheese on top.
  4. Serve immediately.

Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices bread, toasted
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 tbsp basil, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Procedure:

  1. Mix tomatoes, basil, garlic, and oil.
  2. Spoon onto toasted bread.
  3. Drizzle with more oil if needed.
  4. Serve as a starter.

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 12 button mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup cheese, grated
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter

Procedure:

  1. Remove stems from mushrooms.
  2. Mix cheese, breadcrumbs, and garlic.
  3. Fill caps and top with butter.
  4. Bake at 375°F for 15 minutes.
